Frequently Asked Questions

How to delete the account?

To delete your account, tap the menu icon in the top-right corner, open the Account Deletion section, and follow the steps to permanently remove your account.

How to submit my work for review?

Open Assignment Help, tap Provide Your Work, upload your assignment papers or files, and select a date for the review process.

Can I delete my submission?

No. Once you submit your work for review, you cannot edit or delete the submission. However, you can unsubmit it by tapping the Unsubmit button.
