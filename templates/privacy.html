Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 21, 2026

Welcome to the Doit Student Helper App ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application designed for peer-to-peer academic review and study assistance.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum data required to provide you with secure app functionality and maintain platform security.

When you create an account, we collect the username and password you choose. We do not require or collect your email address or phone number. We automatically detect and collect your unique device ID when you access or interact with the app. We also collect the academic task guidelines, reference briefs, or documents you intentionally upload to the platform so that independent mentors can review them and provide study feedback.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data strictly for app functionality and security. We use your information to authenticate your identity, allow you to log in securely, and protect your account. Your device ID is used to enforce security measures, prevent the creation of fraudulent or fake accounts, and ensure platform safety.

We also use your data to display your personal study dashboard, track your active task milestones, and enable you to access external communication channels such as WhatsApp to connect with independent mentors.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your usernames, encrypted passwords, device identifiers, and uploaded files are securely transferred off your device and stored in our centralized database hosted on secure cloud infrastructure.

We implement standard industry security practices to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of your account credentials. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Sharing and Tracking

We do not include third-party advertising networks, analytics software, or data brokers. Your data is never sold, traded, or shared with outside companies for marketing purposes.

Our app contains links to external social messaging platforms such as WhatsApp to facilitate communication. Once you leave our app via these links, your data is governed by the privacy policies of those respective third-party platforms.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your account identifiers, device data, and task files for as long as your account remains active to provide you with continuous service. If you wish to delete your account and remove your data from our database, you can request data deletion directly by contacting us at the support channels provided below.
